Front Foods is a vendor of Pereskia aculeata (Miller), Curcuma longa and Zingiber officinale.

Pereskia aculeata Miller is a native cactus that can be found in Brazil and is called ‘ora-pro-nobis’ (OPN).

OPN leaves showed remarkable levels of total dietary fiber (39.1% dry basis), minerals (calcium, magnesium, manganese and zinc) and vitamins (vitamin A, vitamin C and folic acid).
Among amino acids, tryptophan was the most abundant (20.5% of the total amino acids) and sodium dodecyl sulfate-polyacrylamide gel electrophoresis showed small peptides, inferior to 6.5 kDa, and four major bands (61 kDa, 53 kDa, 33 kDa, and 15 kDa).
